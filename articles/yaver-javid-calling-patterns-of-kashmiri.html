Called Patterns Of Kashmiri
Author : Yaver Javid

Calling is indicated by adding a suffix at the end of the name.

  1. If ending vowel exists, it's usually removed.
  2. For the masculine names suffix -o is added.
  3. For the Feminine names suffixes -ï and -ä are added. Prefrence is usually up to the speaker but often times you find words where only one suffix will work. eg mummy only takes mummä not mummï or zeba only takes zëbï not zëbä.
  4. Southern Kashmiri speakers use the -o suffix for both but still understand and differentiate the other two suffixes.
  5. Usually Both -ä & -ï are tolerated if ending is j, m or y.
  6. In other occasions only -ï is tolorated. Though at the word level exceptions exist.
