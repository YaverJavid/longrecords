Kashmiri Poetry
Author : Yaver Javid

boony cxahy fikri trovus, chu taruk cxender son nishaan
zoony gaashan nindri povus, chu boony wcther son nishaan

scnis watnas duhä vande aos krutth
schine wande pat chu myootth sc'nte cxither son nishaan

maedanav kaetya khä scny jawaan,
tehzen maajan hü'nd chu sabr son nishaan

agar aasan wany kenh doh taqdeeras
wumeed chi rozy khayaalan hü'nd thazar son nishaan


Walo Gulov Gulzaar vandï
Be panni Diluk Schehjaar vandï

Hük ne më az taam kaesi vanith
Az be tchany kheatre Izhaar vandï

Dine kheatre chim ne Haar te Dyaar
Agar cxe vanakh, paneny Schar vandï

Matä cxe katä aaesekh fëraan
Be Sabre saan cxë Intezaar vandï

Chëkh kya më azmavaan, cxe hukm kar
Be panun paan cxë Jaa'nnisar vandï

Az chus be safras manz kuny
Yikhna, be panun paan be Raazdaar vandï
